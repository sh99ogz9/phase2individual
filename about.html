About LumoSave

We're a socially and environmentally conscious solar home system (SHS) provider that strives to serve the "energy poor" of the world. Our unique model combines the innovation of renewable solar technology with the financial accessibility of savings groups, such as VSLAs.

If you’re part of a savings group and would like one of our representatives to come out to your group’s savings meeting, please contacts us or register an account for your group online.

If you’re not part of a savings group but would like to start one, please contact us.

We look forward to serving you for a brighter future.
